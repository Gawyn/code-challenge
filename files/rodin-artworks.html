    About

    Description

    François Auguste René Rodin was a French sculptor generally considered the founder of modern sculpture. He was schooled traditionally and took a craftsman-like approach to his work. Rodin possessed a unique ability to model a complex, turbulent, and deeply pocketed surface in clay. Wikipedia
    Born: November 12, 1840, Paris, France
    Died: November 17, 1917 (age 77 years), Meudon, France
    Periods: Impressionism, Modern art
    Spouse: Rose Beuret (m. 1917–1917)
    Full name: François-Auguste-René Rodin
    Children: Auguste-Eugène Beuret
